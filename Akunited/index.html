She lives her word by trying all she could to ensure that everyone that come in contact with her has his/her lives transform in a very positive way. Madam Lois is a multi-dimensional business woman, a nurse by training and an entrepreneur by calling. She has recorded outstanding feats in the area of networking marketing, health care, skill empowerment to youth at all level across the length and breath of the society. When it comes to business, she has an eagle mindset and can inspired anyone willing to listen to greater height.
